Privacy Policy for Restaurant POS

Effective Date: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Restaurant POS is designed to help restaurants manage their operations locally on your device. We collect and process the following information:

  • Menu Information: Menu items, categories, prices, and descriptions you create
  • Order Data: Customer orders, payment information, and transaction records
  • User Accounts: Staff usernames and access levels for system security
  • Business Data: Restaurant settings, table configurations, and operational preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

All data is stored locally on your device and is used solely for:

  • Processing and managing restaurant orders
  • Generating receipts and reports
  • Managing menu items and pricing
  • Controlling user access and permissions
  • Providing operational analytics and insights

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using secure database encryption. We do not transmit your business data to external servers unless you explicitly choose to use cloud backup features.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal or business data with third parties. Your restaurant data remains private and under your control.

5. Device Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To scan barcodes and take photos of menu items
  • Photo Library: To select images for menu items
  • Bluetooth: To connect to wireless printers and payment devices
  • Local Network: To connect to printers and other devices on your network

6. Data Retention

Data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it. You have full control over your data and can export or delete it at any time through the app settings.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for business use and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data through the app interface
  • Modify or delete your data at any time
  • Export your data for backup purposes
  • Disable specific features or permissions
